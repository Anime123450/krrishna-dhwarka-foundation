Our Story

Krrishna Dhwarka Foundation Bharat was established with a singular vision — to be a beacon of light in the lives of those who are deprived of quality education and opportunities.

Inspired by the divine wisdom of Lord Krishna and the sacred heritage of Dwarka, our foundation works relentlessly to bring the transformative power of education to every corner of Bharat.

Our motto "शिक्षा का प्रकाश" — meaning "Light of Education" — reflects our unwavering commitment to illuminating minds and transforming lives through knowledge.

"The foundation of every state is the education of its youth." — Our guiding principle
Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide quality education and empowerment opportunities to underprivileged children and communities, ensuring that financial constraints never become a barrier to knowledge and growth. We bridge the gap between aspiration and opportunity.

Our Vision

A Bharat where every citizen, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, healthcare, and equal opportunities. A truly enlightened nation rooted in its cultural values, progressing with unity and purpose.

Our Core Values

Education First

We believe education is the cornerstone of individual and societal progress.

Integrity

Every donation is used responsibly with full transparency and accountability.

Compassion

We serve with a heart full of empathy and a commitment to human dignity.

Sustainability

Our programs create lasting change that empowers communities for generations.

What We Do

Our programs create real, lasting impact across India

Scholarship Programme

Annual scholarships for meritorious students from low-income families to pursue their dreams in higher education.

School Supplies Drive

Distribution of books, uniforms, stationery and school bags to students who cannot afford them.

Digital Literacy

Computer training and digital skills programs preparing youth for the 21st-century economy.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training, self-help groups and entrepreneurship support for women from rural communities.

Free Health Camps

Regular medical camps with doctors, medicines, and health screenings for underserved communities.

Cultural Heritage

Promotion and preservation of India's rich artistic and cultural traditions through events and workshops.
