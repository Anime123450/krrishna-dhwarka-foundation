Who We Are

"Education is the most powerful weapon you can use to change the world."

Krrishna Dhwarka Foundation Bharat is a charitable organisation dedicated to the noble mission of spreading the light of knowledge and education to underprivileged communities across India.

Founded on the timeless principles of Dharma and service to humanity, we work tirelessly to create opportunities for those who need it most.

Our Pillars

Guided by purpose, driven by compassion

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality education and empowerment opportunities to underprivileged children and communities, ensuring no child is left behind due to financial constraints.

🌟

Our Vision

A Bharat where every citizen has access to education, healthcare and equal opportunity — a truly enlightened nation rooted in its cultural values.

🙏

Our Values

Truth, Compassion, Integrity and Service. We are inspired by the teachings of Lord Krishna — wisdom, courage, and selfless action for the greater good.

Our Programs

Initiatives making a real difference in people's lives

📚

Education Support

Providing scholarships, books, uniforms and school supplies to underprivileged students across rural and urban India.

🏫

Skill Development

Vocational training programs that equip youth with marketable skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

🌱

Community Welfare

Health camps, women empowerment drives, and rural development initiatives touching thousands of lives.

🏆

Merit Awards

Recognising and rewarding academic excellence with scholarships and awards to inspire the next generation.

🎭

Cultural Preservation

Preserving and promoting India's rich cultural heritage, art, music, and traditions for future generations.

💊

Healthcare Camps

Free medical camps and health awareness programs in underserved communities to improve quality of life.
